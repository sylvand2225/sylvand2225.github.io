History

Hello. My name is Sylvanus Kwesi P. Dadzie. I founded Kwesone Comics in 2015 in New York City.

I have been interested in comics since I was kid and I've also been interested in computer-generated images.

I thought to myself, what if the two can be combined. Why not use the computer to make comic book art. So I decided to take this challenge and soon after Kwesone Comics was born.

I take on this challenge of making all of my comic art with only cgi software by using my weapon of choice, the free, open-source cgi software, Blender.

My journey begins with my first comic book, War Armor. Please enjoy and thank you all.
